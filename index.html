The unofficial site for the village of LA LOUPE, France.

Tucked away in the Eure-et-Loir, at the threshhold of the Perche forest, you will find the quaint French village called LA LOUPE. It is peopled by a mix of civilised Gauls who are invaded twice every year by a hoard of baby-coders, known as "Wilders". According to legend, some Wilders killed themselves with their bad code and they became zombies. LogIn to this site to learn more about the "Walking Dead" in La Loupe.
